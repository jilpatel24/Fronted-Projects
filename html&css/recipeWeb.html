1. Cookie

Ingredients :

  • - sugar
  • - Cocoa powder
  • - Chocolate chips
  • - Almonds

Process :

Step 1 : Freeze nutella ballsLine a baking tray with parchment paper. Then add 12 dollops of Nutella separately on the paper. Let it freeze for half an hour.These Nutella dollops will form Nutella balls.


Step 2 : Make the cookie dough In a bowl, add butter and sugar. Beat until you get a smooth mixture. Then add vanilla extract and egg. Beat again. Make sure the mixture is smooth. Now, add baking soda, cornflour, cocoa powder, and all-purpose flour. Stir to combine well. Form a cookie dough. Add milk if the cookie dough is too dry.


step 3 : Stuff the cookie with nutella Preheat the oven to 180C. After that, take a tablespoon of cookie dough and flatten it. Then add frozen Nutella balls in the center. Wrap the cookie dough around the Nutella ball. Roll it into a ball and then place it on the parchment paper-lined on a baking tray. Repeat the step for other Nutella balls as well.


Step 4 : Bake the cookiesBake the cookies for about 10 mins. Once done, take them out and serve them hot. Your delicious Nutella Chocolate Cookies are ready.
